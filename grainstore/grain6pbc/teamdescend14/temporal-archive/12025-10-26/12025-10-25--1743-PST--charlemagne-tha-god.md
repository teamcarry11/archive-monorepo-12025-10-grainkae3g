## Media Strategy for Grain Network

### **Learn from The Breakfast Club**

1. **Long-form content** (not soundbites)
   - Comprehensive READMEs (not brief)
   - 827-line vision documents (not tweets)
   - 4-version documentation (accessibility)

2. **Cultural authenticity** (not corporate speak)
   - Trish voice (bubbly, real, loving)
   - Glow voice (serious, direct, contemplative)
