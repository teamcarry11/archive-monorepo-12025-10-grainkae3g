## Bernard Arnault's 7 Principles Applied

### 1. Portfolio of Excellence
**LVMH**: 75+ brands, each best in category  
**Grain Network**: 14 teams, each perfect in domain

**Quality > Quantity**:
- Not 40 mediocre teams (like 40-song albums)
- 14 excellent teams (like Ye's 14 songs > 40 philosophy)
- Each team has clear domain, no overlap, no dilution

### 2. Shared Infrastructure
**LVMH**: Corporate services (finance, legal, HR, logistics)  
**Grain Network**: grain06pbc infrastructure

**Shared services**:
- `grainbarrel`: Build system (all teams use)
- `grainflow`: Deployment pipeline (all teams deploy through)

- `graintime`: Temporal awareness (all teams timestamp with)
