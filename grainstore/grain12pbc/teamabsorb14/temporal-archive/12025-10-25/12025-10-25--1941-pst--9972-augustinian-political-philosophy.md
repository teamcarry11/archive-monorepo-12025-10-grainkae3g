## St. Augustine's City of God

### **The Two Cities**

**City of God** (Civitas Dei):
- Love of God to contempt of self
- Eternal, spiritual, heavenly oriented
- Founded on charity, truth, justice
- Citizens are pilgrims (not permanent residents)

**City of Man** (Civitas Terrena):
- Love of self to contempt of God
- Temporal, material, earthly oriented
- Founded on power, domination, pride
- Citizens seek earthly glory

**Not geographic**: Both "cities" exist in every actual city. Spiritual states, not locations.

**The tension**: Christians live IN City of Man while belonging TO City of God.
