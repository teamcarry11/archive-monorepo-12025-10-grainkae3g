### **1. "Designing from the Land" = Boot-from-Scratch Philosophy**

**Backen's approach**:
- Study the land first (topography, soil, climate, views)
- Let building emerge from place (not imposed on it)
- Use local materials (stone from property, local wood)
- Respect what's there (trees, natural features, existing paths)
