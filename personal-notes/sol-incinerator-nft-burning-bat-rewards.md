# SOL Incinerator - NFT Burning for BAT Rewards

**Date**: 2025-10-27 01:38 PDT  
**Platform**: Solana blockchain  
**Website**: https://sol-incinerator.com  
**Rewards**: BAT (Basic Attention Token)

## What is SOL Incinerator?

A decentralized application (dApp) on the Solana blockchain that allows users to clean their wallets by "burning" (permanently destroying) unwanted tokens and NFTs.

**Core Function**: Irreversible token/NFT burning

## How It Works

1. **Connect Wallet**: Connect Solana wallet to sol-incinerator.com
2. **Scan Assets**: dApp automatically scans wallet for unwanted assets (scam NFTs, low-value tokens, spam)
3. **Select Items**: Choose which tokens/NFTs to burn
4. **Burn**: Permanently destroy selected assets
5. **Reclaim**: Get back SOL storage rent fees
6. **Earn Rewards**: Sometimes earn BAT through community "burn-off" events

## Benefits

**Wallet Cleanup**:
- Remove scam NFTs cluttering wallet
