## Multi-Chain Strategy (Revisited)

**From previous docs**: ICP, Hedera, Solana for different layers

**Applied**:
- **Solana** (consumer micropayments): Tips, small purchases, fast/cheap
- **ICP** (retail transactions): Point-of-sale, loyalty tokens, moderate scale
- **Hedera** (wholesale/enterprise): Large B2B transactions, supply chain provenance

**Why multi-chain?**:
- Different use cases need different properties (speed vs finality vs cost)
- Network effect (users on one chain can interact with another)
- Risk mitigation (not dependent on single blockchain)

## Risks & Mitigation

**Risk 1**: Vegan stores may not want new tech (happy with Square)  
**Mitigation**: Offer as free/open-source, no lock-in, they can self-host

**Risk 2**: Too early (crypto adoption still low)  
**Mitigation**: Support both fiat + crypto (hybrid model)

**Risk 3**: No real estate ownership (can't make long-term commitments)  
**Mitigation**: Partner with stores that DO own (Helen definitely owns farm)

**Risk 4**: Building too much too fast (scope creep)  
**Mitigation**: Start with ONE partner (Helen or Besties), prove concept, then expand
