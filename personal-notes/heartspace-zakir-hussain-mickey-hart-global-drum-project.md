# Heartspace - Zakir Hussain & Mickey Hart

**Date**: 2025-10-27  
**Album**: Global Drum Project (2007)  
**Artists**: Mickey Hart, Zakir Hussain, Sikiru Adepoju, Giovanni Hidalgo

## Key Information

**Heartspace** is a track from the Global Drum Project album, featuring world-renowned percussionists collaborating across cultural traditions.

**Global Drum Project**:
- Released: October 2, 2007 (Shout! Factory)
- Won: 2009 Grammy Award for Best Contemporary World Music Album
- Chart: #6 on Billboard World Music Albums
- Duration: 8 songs, 42 minutes

## The Artists

**Mickey Hart**: Grateful Dead drummer, musicologist studying drumming traditions worldwide

**Zakir Hussain**: Master tabla player, Indian classical music virtuoso

**Sikiru Adepoju**: Nigerian talking drum (dundun) master

**Giovanni Hidalgo**: Puerto Rican conguero, Latin percussion legend

## Musical Approach

The album blends:
- Traditional drums and instruments from around the world
- Human voices singing and chanting
- Electronic samples and digital sounds
- Musicological research + modern computer technology
